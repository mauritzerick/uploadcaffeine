## Tips

- **Timing**: Launch Tuesday-Thursday for max visibility
- **Engagement**: Respond to ALL comments quickly
- **Metrics**: Share interesting numbers (performance, conversions)
- **Story**: Emphasize the "why" and learning journey
- **Visuals**: Include screenshots, GIFs, videos
- **Call to Action**: Clear, but not pushy
